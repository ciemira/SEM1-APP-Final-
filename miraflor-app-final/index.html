About UST Book Shop

What is our goal?

Our goal is to provide Thomasians with top notch quality books that we can provide through buying and selling transactions. UST BookShop is a small e commerce web app that can help distribute books to Thomasians This template can be viewed in tablets and mobile devices.

Credit goes to AC Miraflor, daughter of Awesomeness and Cool for creating the prototype and the actual web app itself. This small web app company was inspired by the Facebook Group: UST Book Market -where student interact with each other to sell their books.
